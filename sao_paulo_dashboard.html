Coverage note: OECD provides Brazilian regional data only at the state (TL2) level. This dashboard therefore covers the State of São Paulo (~46M residents — Brazil's largest state by population and GDP, accounting for ~30% of national GDP). City-of-São-Paulo specific unemployment and salary data are not available via free public API. National financial markets are excluded since they are not São Paulo-specific.
